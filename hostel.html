Hostel facilites

Our School has facilities for residential students, our hostel can accommodate a total of 104 students in 26 rooms. Everyday there are 3 meals available for each student, the food menu is designed care of health in mind so that students can stay healthy. Residential students are monitored for prayers, so that they can become pious. All students should have to maintain a routine, so they have done everything on time and they will learn the importance of time. There are a few teachers for residential students who take care of the students' studies and solve the problems of the students. Lastly we can say that our residential system is better than many other madrasas and is suitable for students, so you can keep your child safe here.
